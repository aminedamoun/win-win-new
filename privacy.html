2) Personal Data We Process

a) Contact (enquiry / call / email / form)

First and last name (if provided), email, phone, message content, date/time of communication.

b) Job Application

Data from the application, CV and attachments (e.g. education, experience, contact), interview notes, scheduling agreements.

c) Newsletter

Email (and name if provided), consent record, subscription/unsubscription date, open/click statistics (if you consent to marketing analytics).

d) Technical Data on Site Visit

IP address, cookie and similar technology identifiers, device/browser data, events (e.g. page view, click, form submission) – only if you provide consent.

3) Purposes and Legal Bases

  • Contact and enquiries: pre-contractual measures or communication (GDPR 6(1)(b)) and/or legitimate interest (GDPR 6(1)(f)).
  • Job application: selection process (GDPR 6(1)(b) and/or 6(1)(f)).
  • Newsletter/marketing: consent (GDPR 6(1)(a)); you may unsubscribe at any time.
  • Analytics (Google Analytics): consent (GDPR 6(1)(a)); analytics cookies are not essential and require prior active consent.
  • Advertising/remarketing/conversion tracking ("pixels"): consent (GDPR 6(1)(a)); marketing tracking technologies always require consent.
  • Essential site operation and security: legitimate interest (GDPR 6(1)(f)).

4) Cookies, GTM and "Pixels" – How Consent Works

We use a consent management mechanism (cookie banner) that allows Accept / Reject / Settings and later changes. Pre-placing non-essential cookies ("opt-out") is not permitted; only essential cookies are allowed.

Tracking and tags may be loaded:

  • via Google Tag Manager (GTM) and/or
  • directly in code and/or via GoHighLevel.

Regardless of the method, analytics and marketing are only activated after your consent.

5) Systems / Recipients of Data

  • Hosting: GoDaddy (processor).
  • CRM/forms/newsletter/automations: HighLevel (GoHighLevel) (processor).
  • Analytics: Google Analytics (Google Ireland) – with consent.
  • Advertising/measurement (with consent):
    • Meta Pixel (Meta Platforms Ireland)
    • Google Ads (Google Ireland)
    • LinkedIn Insight Tag (LinkedIn)
    • TikTok Pixel (TikTok)

6) Joint Controllership (Meta)

For certain Meta business technologies (e.g. Pixel), part of the processing is defined as joint processing and is subject to the Meta Controller Addendum.

7) Transfers Outside EU/EEA

Some providers may transfer data outside the EU/EEA. In such cases, appropriate safeguards (e.g. standard contractual clauses) are used in accordance with GDPR.

8) Retention Periods

  • Contact/enquiries: up to 12 months from last communication, then deletion/anonymisation, unless legally required otherwise.
  • Job applications: up to 6 months after conclusion of the process (unless the candidate explicitly consents to longer retention).
  • Newsletter: until consent is withdrawn (unsubscription).
  • Consent records (cookies/marketing): up to 5 years (for accountability).
  • Cookies: until expiry of individual cookies (described in the Cookie Policy).

9) Your Rights

Access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, objection, withdrawal of consent (does not affect lawfulness before withdrawal).

Complaint: Information Commissioner of the Republic of Slovenia.
