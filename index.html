Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about joining Win Win

01Do I need previous sales experience?
No. Previous sales experience is an advantage, but it is not mandatory. We provide structured onboarding, scripts, and continuous coaching. What matters most is motivation, discipline, and the willingness to learn.
02What type of sales will I be doing?
You will work in B2C sales, either door-to-door field sales or call center sales. You will sell telecommunications services, including Internet, TV, and Mobile contracts for end customers.
03Is this a full-time position or freelance work?
Both options are possible. Depending on your profile and agreement, you can work as a full-time employee or as a freelancer (s.p. / company collaboration).
04What are the working hours?
Working hours are structured and clearly defined. Field and call center schedules are organized to support productivity and work-life balance. Specific schedules are discussed during the interview.
05How does payment work?
You receive a base salary or hourly rate plus performance-based commission. Your earnings depend directly on your results. High performers can achieve above-average monthly income.
06How quickly can I advance within the company?
Career progression is performance-based. Successful Sales Advisors can advance to Senior Sales Advisor, Sales Team Leader, or Sales Manager. We actively promote from within.
07Do you provide training and support?
Yes. You receive structured onboarding, sales scripts and CRM tools, monthly coaching and mentoring, and regular sales training and feedback sessions. You are never left alone.
08Where are your offices located?
We operate from offices in Trzin and Kranj. Field sales cover all of Slovenia.
09Is remote work possible?
For experienced candidates and leadership positions, remote or hybrid work may be possible. This is evaluated individually.
10What is the selection process?
The process includes: online application, personal interview & sales simulation, 14-day selection and onboarding program, and contract signing and long-term cooperation.
11What kind of people are you looking for?
We are looking for people who are competitive by nature, goal-oriented, positive and disciplined, motivated to grow professionally and financially, and ready to take responsibility for their success.
12What makes Win Win different from other sales companies?
We combine clear structure, honest communication, strong coaching culture, long-term career opportunities, and a results-driven but supportive environment. At Win Win, success is not luck — it is a system.

Win Win d.o.o. – Your Opportunity for a Successful Career in Sales

Are you looking for a sales job in Ljubljana, Kranj, or other cities across Slovenia? Win Win d.o.o. is one of the most effective Slovenian direct sales companies, offering exceptional opportunities for sales employment, career development, and personal growth. Our sales team operates throughout Slovenia and offers competitive conditions for all who want to build a successful career in sales.

Sales Employment – Best Job Opportunities in Slovenia

Employment in the Win Win sales team means more than just a job – it means entering a dynamic environment where results, perseverance, and ambition are valued. We offer business opportunities in sales for beginners without experience, as well as for experienced sales professionals who want to advance to leadership positions. Our sales company operates in various segments: direct field sales (B2C sales), promotional sales in shopping centers, call center sales, and B2B business sales of telecommunications services to end users.

If you're looking for employment in Ljubljana, Maribor, Celje, Kranj, or any other city in Slovenia, we are the right choice. Win Win d.o.o. employs salespeople, team leaders, team managers, sales managers, and other sales professionals at various locations throughout Slovenia. Our offices are located in Slovenia's largest cities.

Sales Position – Opportunities for All Generations

The sales position at Win Win is suitable for students looking for student work in sales, for young people without work experience who want to gain sales experience, as well as for experienced sales professionals who want to upgrade their knowledge and earnings. We offer flexible forms of employment: full-time work, cooperation through a company (sole proprietorship, LLC), student work, and the possibility of working from home for certain call center positions.

Employment as a salesperson at Win Win d.o.o. enables you to earn a high income that depends on your results. Our commission reward system means the more you sell, the more you earn. The best salespeople in our team achieve monthly earnings that exceed the Slovenian average, proving that a sales position can be extremely profitable for those willing to invest effort and energy.

Career in Sales – From Salesperson to Manager

Win Win d.o.o. offers a clear career path for all individuals in sales. You can start as a sales consultant in the field or in a call center, then advance to sales team leader, team leader, sales manager, or even regional director. Our company actively invests in education and training, which means you will acquire sales knowledge, communication skills, sales techniques, negotiation skills, and leadership competencies with us.

Regular sales training, workshops, seminars, and coaching sessions are part of our employee development culture. Employees at Win Win d.o.o. have the opportunity to obtain certificates in telecommunications sales, leadership, and management, which strengthens their competitiveness in the labor market.

Win Win Sales Team – Culture of Success and Teamwork

Our sales team consists of ambitious individuals who share common values: perseverance, honesty, results, and team spirit. We operate in a dynamic and motivating environment where every success is celebrated, every sale counts, and every team member contributes to the common goal. Win Win d.o.o. organizes regular team buildings, motivational events, awards for the best salespeople, and annual meetings where we celebrate collective successes.

A position in the Win Win sales team is not just employment – it's a lifestyle, an opportunity for personal growth, financial independence, and a long-term career in one of the fastest-growing sectors in Slovenia.

If you're an experienced salesperson and looking for a challenge in business sales, we invite you to apply. B2B positions at Win Win offer high commissions, access to larger projects, the possibility of building long-term business relationships, and advancement to leadership positions.

Student Work in Sales – First Employment for Students

For students who show good results, we offer the possibility of employment after completing their studies, advancement to leadership positions, and a long-term career in our company. Student work at Win Win is an excellent stepping stone for a successful career in sales or marketing.

Position Without Experience – All Beginners Welcome
