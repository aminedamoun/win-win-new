Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about joining Win Win

What makes Win Win different from other sales agencies?
We combine clear structure, honest communication, strong coaching culture, long-term career opportunities, and a results-driven but supportive environment. At Win Win, success is not luck — it is a system.
Do you provide training for newcomers to sales?
Yes! We provide comprehensive onboarding including product knowledge, sales scripts, CRM training, and ongoing coaching. Many of our top performers started with no prior sales experience.
What kind of support do you offer?
We provide CRM tools, proven scripts, back-office support, regular coaching sessions, performance tracking, and a supportive team environment. You're never alone in your sales journey.
How is compensation structured?
Our compensation models vary by role but always include performance-based earnings. We offer competitive base salaries or hourly rates plus attractive commission structures with uncapped earning potential.
What career progression opportunities exist?
Top performers can advance to senior sales roles, team leadership positions, or specialized B2B consultancy. We promote from within and reward consistent excellence.
Do you work with both B2C and B2B clients?
Yes, we operate across multiple channels: field sales (B2C), call center operations (B2C), and business consulting (B2B). This diversity offers varied career paths for different skill sets and preferences.
What kind of people are you looking for?
We look for people who are naturally competitive, goal-oriented, positive and disciplined, motivated by professional and financial growth, and ready to take ownership of their own success.
What does the selection process look like?
The process includes: an online application, a personal interview and sales simulation, a 14-day selection and onboarding programme, and signing a contract for long-term collaboration.
Is remote or hybrid work possible?
For experienced candidates and leadership positions, remote or hybrid work may be an option. This is assessed on an individual basis.
Where are your offices located?
We operate from offices in Trzin and Kranj. Our field sales team covers the whole of Slovenia.
Do you provide training and support?
Yes. You receive structured onboarding, sales scripts and CRM tools, monthly coaching and mentorship, and regular sales training with ongoing feedback. You are never left on your own.
